1. Introduction

Welcome to Wordling.org ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website wordling.org and play our word games.

By using our website and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our website:

a. Personal Information

  • Contact information (such as email address) when you voluntarily provide it for notifications or account registration
  • Game preferences and settings you select

b. Non-Personal Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (type, operating system, screen resolution)
  • IP address (anonymized and used for analytics only)
  • Game performance data (scores, progress, difficulty preferences)
  • Usage patterns and gameplay statistics

c. Automatically Collected Information

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your interactions with our website. For more details, see our Cookies section.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:cite[1]:cite[6]:

  • To provide and maintain our word game services
  • To personalize your gaming experience and suggest appropriate difficulty levels
  • To analyze and improve our website and game performance
  • To monitor usage patterns and trends to enhance user experience
  • To develop new features and game variants
  • To prevent misuse and ensure security of our website
  • To communicate with you about updates, features, or promotional offers (if you've opted in)

4. Data Storage and Retention

a. Storage Methods

We store your information using secure methods including:cite[1]:

  • Local storage (browser caching) for game progress and preferences
  • Secure databases for user data (if applicable)
  • Server logs for security and analytics purposes

b. Retention Period

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:cite[1]:cite[6]:

  • Game progress and statistics: Retained indefinitely unless you request deletion
  • Analytics data: Retained for 26 months
  • Server logs: Retained for 90 days

c. Data Location

All data collected is stored on servers located in the United States. We do not currently transfer or store your information outside of the United States:cite[1].

5. Data Protection and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:cite[1]:cite[6]. These measures include:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security assessments and testing
  • Access controls limiting who can access user data
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data:cite[1].

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and store certain information:cite[1]. The technologies we use include:

a. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your game settings and preferences
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website

b. Cookie Management

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service, such as saving your game progress.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:cite[1]:cite[6]:cite[10]:

  • Access: The right to request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • Erasure: The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: The right to request limiting the processing of your personal data
  • Objection: The right to object to our processing of your personal data
  • Data Portability: The right to request transfer of your data to another organization
  • Withdraw Consent: The right to withdraw consent where we relied on it to process your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is designed for a general audience and is appropriate for children interested in word games. However:cite[1]:cite[6]:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us
  • We encourage parents to monitor their children's online activities and consider using parental control tools

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date:cite[1]:cite[6].

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:cite[1]:cite[6]:

  • Email: privacy@wordling.org
  • Contact Form: wordling.org/contact

We will respond to all legitimate inquiries within 30 days.
